## üìä What to Keep PROPRIETARY (Your Secret Sauce)

### üîí Tier 1: Core IP (Never Open Source)

#### 1. **Trained ML Models & Datasets**
- **What:** Your collected telemetry data, trained models, proprietary thresholds
- **Why:** This is your data moat - gets stronger over time
- **Protection:** 
  - Store in encrypted cloud
  - License agreements
  - Terms of service for data usage
- **Value:** High - improves with more users

#### 2. **Advanced Analytics Algorithms**
- **What:** Custom algorithms beyond standard libraries
- **Why:** Competitive differentiation
- **Protection:** Patent key algorithms (if novel)
- **Value:** Medium-High

#### 3. **Cloud Platform & Aggregated Data**
- **What:** Performance benchmarks, community data, predictive models
- **Why:** Network effects - more users = better insights
- **Protection:** Server-side only, API access control
- **Value:** Very High - creates moat

#### 4. **Proprietary ECU Mappings**
- **What:** Custom DBC files, ECU-specific configurations
- **Why:** Hard-won knowledge, competitive advantage
- **Protection:** Encrypted, licensed per vehicle
- **Value:** High

### üîí Tier 2: Strategic IP (Consider Licensing)

#### 5. **Hardware Optimization Code**
- **What:** reTerminal DM specific optimizations
- **Why:** Hardware partnership advantage
- **Protection:** Keep proprietary, license to hardware partners
- **Value:** Medium

#### 6. **Professional Tuning Workflows**
- **What:** Step-by-step tuning procedures, best practices
- **Why:** Professional services revenue
- **Protection:** Documentation, training materials
- **Value:** Medium

## üõ°Ô∏è IP Protection Strategy

### 1. **Patents** (If Applicable)
- **File for:** Novel algorithms, unique hardware integration methods
- **Cost:** $5,000-$15,000 per patent
- **Timeline:** 18-24 months
- **Recommendation:** File provisional patents now ($2,000), decide on full later

### 2. **Trademarks**
- **File for:** "AI Tuner Agent", logo, tagline
- **Cost:** $250-$500 per class
- **Timeline:** 6-12 months
- **Recommendation:** Do this immediately

### 3. **Copyright**
- **Automatic:** Code, documentation, designs
- **Registration:** $65 per work (optional but recommended)
- **Recommendation:** Register key works before launch

### 4. **Trade Secrets**
- **What:** Proprietary algorithms, training data, business processes
- **Protection:** NDAs, employee agreements, secure storage
- **Recommendation:** Document what's secret, limit access

### 5. **Licensing Agreements**
- **Software License:** EULA for proprietary components
- **Data License:** Terms for data collection/usage
- **Hardware License:** Partnership agreements
- **Recommendation:** Have lawyer review before launch
