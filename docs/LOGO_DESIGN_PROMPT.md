   - Clean, uncluttered overall composition
   - But with intricate, interesting details on closer inspection
   - Balance simplicity with sophistication

   - Sharp, precise lines and edges

   - Subtle 3D effects or isometric perspective
   - Layering and depth
   - Creates premium, professional feel

   - Works in both light and dark contexts
   - Ensures readability at all sizes

- **Gradients**: Subtle, professional gradients (not rainbow)

- **Shadows**: Minimal, subtle depth shadows
- **Particles**: Small data particles or sparks (not overwhelming)
- **Motion Blur**: Subtle speed lines (not heavy blur)
