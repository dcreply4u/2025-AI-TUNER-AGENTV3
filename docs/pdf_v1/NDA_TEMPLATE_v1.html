### RECITALS

WHEREAS, the parties wish to engage in discussions regarding a potential investment/acquisition transaction ("Transaction");

WHEREAS, in connection with such discussions, each party may disclose confidential and proprietary information to the other;

NOW, THEREFORE, the parties agree as follows:

---

### 1. DEFINITION OF CONFIDENTIAL INFORMATION

"Confidential Information" means all non-public, proprietary, or confidential information disclosed by either party to the other, whether orally, in writing, or in any other form, including but not limited to:

**From Disclosing Party:**
- Source code, algorithms, software architecture
- Technical documentation and specifications
- Proprietary algorithms (AI, machine learning models)
- Business plans, financial information, customer data
- Trade secrets, know-how, processes
- Product roadmaps, development plans
- Customer lists, contracts, agreements
- Any information marked "Confidential" or "Proprietary"

**From Receiving Party:**
- Investment criteria, evaluation processes
- Strategic plans, market analysis
- Any information marked "Confidential"

**Exclusions:**
- Information publicly available
- Information independently developed
- Information rightfully received from third party
- Information required by law to be disclosed

---

### 2. PURPOSE

Confidential Information is disclosed solely for the purpose of evaluating the potential Transaction. Receiving Party agrees to use Confidential Information only for this purpose and not for any competitive or other purpose.

---

### 3. OBLIGATIONS OF RECEIVING PARTY

Receiving Party agrees to:

a) **Maintain Confidentiality**: Hold all Confidential Information in strict confidence and not disclose to any third party without prior written consent;

b) **Limited Access**: Restrict access to Confidential Information to employees, advisors, or representatives who:
- Have a need to know for the stated purpose
- Are bound by confidentiality obligations at least as restrictive as this Agreement
- Are informed of the confidential nature of the information

c) **No Reverse Engineering**: Not reverse engineer, decompile, or disassemble any software or technology;

d) **No Competitive Use**: Not use Confidential Information to compete with Disclosing Party or develop competing products;

e) **Return/Destroy**: Upon request or termination of discussions, immediately return or destroy all Confidential Information and certify destruction in writing;

f) **No Copying**: Not make copies except as necessary for the stated purpose;

g) **Marking**: Maintain any confidentiality markings on materials.

---

### 4. DURATION

This Agreement shall remain in effect for **FIVE (5) YEARS** from the date of last disclosure of Confidential Information, except that:
- Trade secrets shall remain protected indefinitely
- Return/destruction obligations survive termination

---

### 5. NO LICENSE OR WARRANTY

Nothing in this Agreement grants any license, right, or interest in Confidential Information. All Confidential Information is provided "AS IS" without warranty.

---

### 6. REMEDIES

Receiving Party acknowledges that:
- Confidential Information is valuable and unique
- Breach would cause irreparable harm
- Money damages would be inadequate
- Disclosing Party is entitled to injunctive relief
- Disclosing Party may seek monetary damages
- Disclosing Party may recover attorney fees and costs

---

### 7. RETURN OF MATERIALS

Upon request or termination of discussions, Receiving Party shall:
- Return all documents, materials, and copies
- Delete all electronic copies
- Certify destruction in writing within 10 days
- Not retain any copies in any form

---

### 8. NO OBLIGATION TO PROCEED

This Agreement does not obligate either party to:
- Proceed with the Transaction
- Enter into any other agreement
- Provide any additional information

---

### 9. GOVERNING LAW

This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of [Your State/Country], without regard to conflict of laws.

---

### 10. ENTIRE AGREEMENT

This Agreement constitutes the entire agreement regarding confidentiality and supersedes all prior agreements.

---

### 11. MODIFICATIONS

This Agreement may only be modified in writing signed by both parties.

---

### 12. SEVERABILITY

If any provision is found unenforceable, the remainder shall remain in effect.

## Additional Clauses to Consider

### Code-Specific Protections

**Add if sharing code:**

```
13. CODE ACCESS RESTRICTIONS

Receiving Party agrees that:
- Code access shall be read-only
- No copying, downloading, or exporting of code
- Access limited to [specific repository/branch]
- Access time-limited to [X days/weeks]
- All access logged and monitored
- No decompiling, reverse engineering, or analysis beyond stated purpose
```

### Watermarking

**Add if using watermarked materials:**

```
14. WATERMARKING

All materials provided are watermarked with Receiving Party's name and date.
Receiving Party agrees not to remove watermarks or alter materials.
```

### Exclusivity (Optional)

**Add if you want exclusivity period:**

```
15. EXCLUSIVITY

Receiving Party agrees to an exclusivity period of [X days] during which
they will not engage in discussions with competitors or other potential
buyers/investors.
```

## One-Way NDA (If They Won't Sign Mutual)

**If investor only wants one-way NDA (protects them, not you):**

**Consider:**
- Is this a red flag?
- Do you have leverage to negotiate?
- Is the opportunity worth it?

**If you must accept:**
- Add clauses protecting your code
- Limit scope of disclosure
- Shorten duration if possible
- Get attorney review

---

## Checklist Before Signing

**Review NDA for:**
- [ ] Mutual protection (or acceptable one-way)
- [ ] Clear definition of Confidential Information
- [ ] Appropriate duration (2-5 years typical)
- [ ] Return/destruction clause
- [ ] Remedies clause (injunctive relief)
- [ ] No reverse engineering clause
- [ ] No competitive use clause
- [ ] Governing law (your jurisdiction preferred)
- [ ] Attorney fees clause
- [ ] Code-specific protections (if sharing code)

---
