# TelemetryIQ - Logo Design Concepts

## Logo Concept Overview

### Design Philosophy
- **Modern**: Tech-forward, contemporary
- **Professional**: Enterprise-grade, trustworthy
- **Intelligent**: Represents AI/analytics
- **Performance**: Racing/automotive aesthetic

## Concept 1: Minimalist Wordmark (RECOMMENDED)

### Design
```
TelemetryIQ
───────────
```

**Description:**
- Clean, professional wordmark
- Custom typography with subtle tech elements
- "IQ" in accent color (electric blue) or bold weight
- Horizontal line below representing data flow

**Colors:**
- Primary: Electric Blue (#00E0FF)
- Text: White on dark background
- Accent: "IQ" in blue

**Usage:**
- Primary logo for all applications
- Works at any size
- Versatile for print and digital

## Concept 2: TIQ Monogram

## Color Specifications

### Primary Logo Colors
- **Electric Blue**: `#00E0FF` (Primary)
- **White**: `#FFFFFF` (Text)
- **Dark Background**: `#0D1117` (Background)

### Accent Colors
- **Racing Red**: `#FF6B6B` (Highlights)
- **Success Green**: `#51CF66` (Positive)
- **Warning Yellow**: `#FFD43B` (Alerts)

### Logo Variations
1. **Full Color**: Blue on dark background
2. **Single Color**: White on dark
3. **Reverse**: Dark on light background
4. **Monochrome**: Black/white

## Typography

### Primary Font
**Inter** or **Roboto** (Modern, tech-forward)

### Logo Typography
- Custom letterforms
- Slight geometric modifications
- "IQ" in distinct weight/color
- Professional, readable

## Usage Guidelines

### Minimum Size
- **Full Logo**: 120px width minimum
- **Icon**: 24px height minimum

### Clear Space
- **2x logo height** on all sides
- Never place other elements closer

### Don't
- ❌ Stretch or distort
- ❌ Rotate or tilt
- ❌ Change colors (except approved variations)
- ❌ Add effects (shadows, gradients on logo itself)
- ❌ Place on busy backgrounds

### Do
- ✅ Use approved color variations
- ✅ Maintain clear space
- ✅ Use appropriate size
- ✅ Place on solid backgrounds
