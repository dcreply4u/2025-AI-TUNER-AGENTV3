### Product Description
**TelemetryIQ** is the next-generation racing telemetry platform that brings enterprise-grade intelligence to performance vehicles. Our AI-powered system analyzes real-time vehicle data to deliver actionable insights that optimize performance, predict failures, and accelerate tuning decisions.

**Key Features:**
- **AI-Powered Analytics**: Machine learning algorithms analyze telemetry data to identify patterns and opportunities
- **Virtual Dyno**: Calculate horsepower from real-world driving (no expensive dyno runs)
- **Predictive Maintenance**: Detect issues before they become problems
- **Auto-Tuning**: AI automatically optimizes ECU parameters based on conditions
- **Enterprise Ready**: Secure, scalable, and designed for professional teams

## Brand Guidelines

### Logo Usage
- **Minimum Size**: 24px height
- **Clear Space**: 2x logo height on all sides
- **Color Variations**: Full color, single color, reverse
- **Don't**: Stretch, rotate, or modify logo

### Color Usage
- **Primary**: Electric blue (#00E0FF) for primary actions
- **Background**: Dark (#0D1117) for tech feel
- **Accents**: Use sparingly for highlights

### Typography
- **Consistent**: Use brand fonts across all materials
- **Hierarchy**: Maintain clear visual hierarchy
- **Readability**: Ensure sufficient contrast
