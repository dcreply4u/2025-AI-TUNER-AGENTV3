## üì¶ Product Tier Definitions

### Tier 1: Base Model
**Target Market:** Entry-level users, DIY enthusiasts  
**Hardware:**
- Raspberry Pi 5 (4GB RAM)
- Single CAN Bus HAT (MCP2515)
- GPS HAT (NEO-6M or similar)
- Basic GPIO access (built-in)
- Power supply
- Case/enclosure
- MicroSD card (32GB)

### Tier 2: Pro Model
**Target Market:** Serious enthusiasts, amateur racers  
**Hardware:**
- Raspberry Pi 5 (8GB RAM)
- Dual CAN Bus HATs (or dual CAN FD HAT)
- GPS HAT (NEO-M8N or better)
- GPIO Expander HAT (MCP23017)
- IMU/Motion sensor (MPU6050 or BNO085)
- Power supply
- Premium case/enclosure
- MicroSD card (64GB)

### Tier 3: Ultimate Model
**Target Market:** Professional tuners, race teams  
**Hardware:**
- Raspberry Pi 5 (8GB RAM)
- Dual CAN FD HATs (MCP2518FD)
- High-precision GPS HAT (NEO-M8N or better)
- Dual GPIO Expander HATs
- IMU/Motion sensor (BNO085 - 9-axis)
- ADC HAT (ADS1115) for analog sensors
- Premium power supply with backup
- Industrial-grade case/enclosure
- High-speed MicroSD card (128GB)
- Optional: M.2 SSD expansion

### Tier 4: Enterprise/Shop Bundle
**Target Market:** Tuning shops, fleet operators  
**Hardware:**
- 5x Pro Model units
- Commercial license
- Team cloud account
- Training materials
- Priority support

## üéØ Key Insights & Recommendations

### 1. Pricing Strategy
- **Base Model:** Strong entry point at $299-399
- **Pro Model:** Best value proposition at $449-599
- **Ultimate Model:** Premium positioning at $649-799
- **Enterprise:** High-margin bundle at $1,999-2,499

### 2. Cost Optimization
- **Bulk Purchasing:** Order 500+ units for best pricing
- **HAT Selection:** Consider all-in-one HATs to reduce assembly
- **Assembly:** Consider automation for 1000+ units
- **Packaging:** Negotiate bulk packaging deals

### 3. Revenue Mix
- **Hardware:** 80% of revenue (primary)
- **Subscriptions:** 15% of revenue (recurring)
- **Services:** 5% of revenue (high margin)

### 4. Break-Even Analysis
- **Fixed Costs (Year 1):** ~$50,000 (development, marketing, operations)
- **Break-Even Units:** ~200 units (all tiers combined)
- **Break-Even Revenue:** ~$100,000

### 5. Risk Factors
- **Component Availability:** Pi 5 may have supply constraints
- **Price Fluctuations:** Component prices can vary
- **Competition:** Market may become competitive
- **Adoption Rate:** Actual sales may be lower than projections
