## üí∞ Bulk Purchasing Tips

### 1. Contact Suppliers Directly
- **Email:** Most suppliers have dedicated wholesale email addresses
- **Phone:** Call for immediate pricing and availability
- **Online Forms:** Many have wholesale inquiry forms on their websites

### 2. Minimum Order Quantities (MOQ)
- **Small Wholesale:** 10-50 units (typically 10-15% discount)
- **Medium Wholesale:** 50-200 units (typically 15-25% discount)
- **Large Wholesale:** 200+ units (typically 25-40% discount)
- **Enterprise:** 1000+ units (custom pricing, dedicated support)

### 3. Pricing Tiers
Most suppliers offer tiered pricing:
- **1-9 units:** Retail pricing
- **10-49 units:** Small volume discount
- **50-199 units:** Medium volume discount
- **200-999 units:** Large volume discount
- **1000+ units:** Enterprise pricing (negotiate)

### 4. What to Ask Suppliers
- Minimum order quantity (MOQ)
- Volume pricing tiers
- Lead times and availability
- Payment terms (net 30, net 60, etc.)
- Shipping costs and options
- Return/exchange policies
- Technical support availability
- Educational/research discounts (if applicable)

### 5. Payment Terms
- **Net 30/60:** Common for established businesses
- **Credit terms:** May require credit application
- **Prepayment:** Often required for first orders
- **Wire transfer:** Common for large orders

## üöÄ Quick Start: Getting Wholesale Pricing

### Step 1: Prepare Your Requirements
```
- Quantity needed (initial + future orders)
- Specific models (Pi 5 4GB, 8GB, etc.)
- HAT types and quantities
- Timeline/deadline
- Budget range
```

### Step 2: Contact Multiple Suppliers
Send inquiry email template:

```
Subject: Wholesale Inquiry - Raspberry Pi 5 & HATs

Hello,

I'm interested in purchasing Raspberry Pi 5 units and compatible HATs 
in wholesale quantities for a commercial application.

Requirements:
- Raspberry Pi 5: [X] units ([4GB/8GB])
- CAN Bus HATs: [X] units
- GPS HATs: [X] units
- GPIO Expander HATs: [X] units

Could you please provide:
- Wholesale pricing for these quantities
- Minimum order quantities
- Lead times
- Payment terms
- Shipping options and costs

Thank you,
[Your Name]
[Company Name]
[Contact Information]
```

### Step 3: Compare Offers
- Compare pricing (unit cost + shipping)
- Evaluate lead times
- Check payment terms
- Review return/exchange policies
- Consider technical support availability

### Step 4: Place Test Order
- Order small quantity first (10-20 units)
- Test quality and compatibility
- Evaluate supplier reliability
- Then scale up to larger orders

## ‚ö†Ô∏è Important Considerations

### Availability
- **Raspberry Pi 5:** May have limited availability initially
- **Lead Times:** Can be 4-12 weeks for large orders
- **Backorders:** Common for popular items

### Quality Control
- **Test samples first:** Order 1-2 units to test before bulk order
- **Check compatibility:** Ensure HATs work with Pi 5 (not all Pi 4 HATs work)
- **Documentation:** Verify HATs have good documentation

### Shipping & Import
- **US Suppliers:** No import duties, faster shipping
- **International:** May have import duties, longer shipping
- **Bulk Shipping:** Negotiate shipping costs for large orders

### Warranty & Support
- **Warranty:** Check warranty terms for bulk orders
- **Support:** Ensure technical support is available
- **Returns:** Understand return policy for defective units
