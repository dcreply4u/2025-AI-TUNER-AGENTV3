## Brand Overview

**TelemetryIQ** is an AI-powered racing telemetry and tuning system that combines cutting-edge artificial intelligence with high-performance automotive technology.

### Brand Values

- **Intelligence**: AI-powered, data-driven, smart solutions
- **Performance**: Racing-grade, speed, optimization
- **Professional**: Enterprise-grade, reliable, trustworthy
- **Innovative**: Cutting-edge, forward-thinking, advanced
- **Precise**: Engineering excellence, accuracy, detail-oriented

### Brand Personality

- **Modern**: Contemporary, tech-forward, sophisticated
- **Dynamic**: Energetic, fast-paced, performance-focused
- **Professional**: Enterprise-ready, credible, trustworthy
- **Premium**: High-quality, polished, refined

## Logo Placement

### Minimum Clear Space

Maintain clear space around logo equal to the height of the "I" letter in the "IQ" center element.

```
┌─────────────────────────┐
│                         │
│    [Clear Space]        │
│    ┌─────────────┐      │
│    │   [Logo]    │      │
│    └─────────────┘      │
│    [Clear Space]        │
│                         │
└─────────────────────────┘
```

### Placement Guidelines

**DO**:
- ✅ Place logo in top-left or top-center of layouts
- ✅ Use on solid color backgrounds
- ✅ Maintain clear space requirements
- ✅ Use appropriate color variation for background

**DON'T**:
- ❌ Place logo in corners or cramped spaces
- ❌ Overlap logo with other elements
- ❌ Place on busy or cluttered backgrounds
- ❌ Rotate or tilt the logo
- ❌ Add effects beyond approved design

## Color Palette

- CMYK: `100, 12, 0, 0`

- RGB: `255, 59, 59`

- Hex: `#0D1117`

## Typography

### Primary Font Family

**Inter** (Recommended)
- **Weights**: Regular (400), Medium (500), SemiBold (600), Bold (700)
- **Usage**: Body text, UI elements, marketing materials
- **Characteristics**: Modern, tech-forward, excellent readability

### Alternative Fonts

**Roboto**
- **Usage**: Android applications, web interfaces
- **Weights**: Regular, Medium, Bold

**Montserrat**
- **Usage**: Headings, display text
- **Weights**: Regular, SemiBold, Bold

### Typography Hierarchy

**H1 - Main Heading**
- Font: Inter Bold
- Size: 48-72px
- Color: Electric Blue (#00E0FF) or White
- Usage: Page titles, hero sections

**H2 - Section Heading**
- Font: Inter SemiBold
- Size: 32-48px
- Color: Electric Blue (#00E0FF) or White
- Usage: Section titles

**H3 - Subsection Heading**
- Font: Inter Medium
- Size: 24-32px
- Color: Electric Blue (#00E0FF) or White
- Usage: Subsection titles

**Body Text**
- Font: Inter Regular
- Size: 16-18px
- Color: White or Light Gray (#E0E0E0)
- Usage: Paragraphs, descriptions

**Small Text**
- Font: Inter Regular
- Size: 14px
- Color: Light Gray (#B0B0B0)
- Usage: Captions, labels, fine print

## Voice & Tone

### Brand Voice

**Professional yet Approachable**
- Technical accuracy without jargon overload
- Confident but not arrogant
- Clear and direct communication

### Tone Guidelines

**For Technical Documentation**:
- Precise, detailed, comprehensive
- Use technical terms appropriately
- Provide clear examples

**For Marketing Materials**:
- Energetic, exciting, performance-focused
- Highlight benefits and capabilities
- Use racing/performance metaphors

**For User Interface**:
- Clear, concise, helpful
- Action-oriented language
- Friendly but professional

### Writing Style

- ✅ Use active voice
- ✅ Be specific and concrete
- ✅ Use short sentences for clarity
- ✅ Break up long paragraphs
- ✅ Use bullet points for lists
- ❌ Avoid jargon without explanation
- ❌ Avoid overly casual language
- ❌ Avoid marketing fluff
