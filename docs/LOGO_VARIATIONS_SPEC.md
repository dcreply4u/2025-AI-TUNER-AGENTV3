# TelemetryIQ Logo Variations Specification

## Required Variations

### 1. Full Color (Primary) ✅
**Status**: Approved  
**Use Case**: Primary logo for all digital and print applications  
**Colors**: 
- Electric blue (#00E0FF)
- Racing red (#FF3B3B)
- Dark background (#0D1117)

---

### 2. White Version
**Status**: To Be Created  
**Use Case**: Dark backgrounds, night mode, dark themes  
**Specifications**:
- Convert all electric blue elements to white/light gray
- Maintain racing red accents (or convert to light red/pink)
- Keep dark background or make transparent
- Preserve 3D depth and glow effects (white glow)
- Ensure visibility on dark backgrounds

**Color Mapping**:
- Electric blue (#00E0FF) → White (#FFFFFF) or Light Gray (#E0E0E0)
- Racing red (#FF3B3B) → Light Red (#FF9999) or Pink (#FFB3D9)
- Dark background → Transparent or Dark (#0D1117)

---

### 3. Black Version
**Status**: To Be Created  
**Use Case**: Light backgrounds, single-color print  
**Specifications**:
- Convert all elements to black/dark gray
- Maintain visual hierarchy and contrast
- Remove color-dependent effects
- Ensure readability on light backgrounds

**Color Mapping**:
- Electric blue (#00E0FF) → Black (#000000) or Dark Gray (#333333)
- Racing red (#FF3B3B) → Black (#000000) or Dark Gray (#333333)
- Dark background → Transparent or White (#FFFFFF)

---

### 4. Reverse (Light Background)
**Status**: To Be Created  
**Use Case**: Light backgrounds, print materials, white paper  
**Specifications**:
- Dark version of logo for light backgrounds
- Maintain all design elements
- Ensure sufficient contrast
- Adjust glow effects for light background

**Color Mapping**:
- Electric blue (#00E0FF) → Dark Blue (#0066CC) or Black (#000000)
- Racing red (#FF3B3B) → Dark Red (#CC0000) or Black (#000000)
- Dark background → Transparent or White (#FFFFFF)

---

### 5. Monochrome/Grayscale
**Status**: To Be Created  
**Use Case**: Single-color printing, fax, low-color applications  
**Specifications**:
- Convert to black and white only
- Maintain visual hierarchy through contrast
- Ensure all elements are distinguishable
- Test at various sizes

**Color Mapping**:
- All colors → Black (#000000) or White (#FFFFFF)
- Use grayscale for depth/shading if needed

---

### 6. Icon Only (No Wordmark)
**Status**: To Be Created  
**Use Case**: Favicon, app icons, small spaces, social media profiles  
**Specifications**:
- Central gauge/tachometer emblem only
- Remove "TELEMETRYIQ" wordmark
- Maintain all gauge elements (checkered flag, rings, "IQ", needle)
- Simplify for small sizes (16x16px, 32x32px)
- Create multiple sizes: 16x16, 32x32, 48x48, 64x64, 128x128, 256x256, 512x512, 1024x1024

**Simplification Guidelines**:
- **16x16px**: Minimal detail, recognizable shape only
- **32x32px**: Basic elements, simplified "IQ"
- **48x48px+**: Full detail, all elements visible

---

### 7. Wordmark Only (No Icon)
**Status**: To Be Created  
**Use Case**: Horizontal spaces, headers, when icon isn't appropriate  
**Specifications**:
- "TELEMETRYIQ" text only
- Maintain electric blue color and 3D depth
- Preserve typography style
- Create horizontal version
