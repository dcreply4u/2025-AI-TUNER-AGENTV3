# First-Time Founder Checklist

## üöÄ Pre-Launch Essentials

### Legal Structure (Do First!)

#### 1. Business Entity
- [ ] **Choose entity type:**
  - LLC (recommended for first-time): Simple, flexible, pass-through taxation
  - Corporation (C-Corp): If planning to raise VC funding
  - S-Corp: Tax benefits, but more restrictions
- [ ] **File with state** ($50-$500 depending on state)
- [ ] **Get EIN** from IRS (free, online)
- [ ] **Set up business bank account**
- [ ] **Get business credit card**

**Timeline:** 1-2 weeks  
**Cost:** $200-$1,000

#### 2. Intellectual Property
- [ ] **Trademark application** ($250-$500)
  - Business name: "AI Tuner Agent"
  - Logo/tagline
  - File with USPTO
- [ ] **Copyright registration** ($65 per work)
  - Key code files
  - Documentation
  - Marketing materials
- [ ] **Provisional patent** (if applicable, $2,000)
  - Novel algorithms
  - Unique methods
- [ ] **Trade secret documentation**
  - List what's proprietary
  - Access controls
  - NDAs

**Timeline:** 2-4 weeks  
**Cost:** $500-$3,000

#### 3. Legal Documents
- [ ] **Terms of Service** (ToS)
  - Use template or lawyer ($500-$2,000)
- [ ] **Privacy Policy**
  - Required for data collection
  - GDPR/CCPA compliance
- [ ] **End User License Agreement** (EULA)
  - Software licensing terms
- [ ] **Non-Disclosure Agreement** (NDA)
  - For beta testers, partners
- [ ] **Consultation with IP lawyer** ($200-$500/hour)
  - Review strategy
  - Answer questions

**Timeline:** 2-3 weeks  
**Cost:** $1,000-$5,000

---

### Financial Setup

#### 4. Accounting & Taxes
- [ ] **Set up accounting system**
  - QuickBooks Online ($25/month)
  - Or Wave (free)
- [ ] **Find accountant** ($500-$2,000/year)
  - For tax prep and advice
- [ ] **Set aside 30% for taxes**
  - Self-employment tax
  - Income tax
- [ ] **Track all expenses**
  - Receipts
  - Mileage
  - Equipment

**Timeline:** 1 week  
**Cost:** $500-$2,000 setup

#### 5. Insurance
- [ ] **General liability insurance** ($500-$2,000/year)
  - Protects against lawsuits
- [ ] **Product liability** (if selling hardware)
- [ ] **Errors & Omissions** (E&O) ($1,000-$3,000/year)
  - For software errors
- [ ] **Business property** (if you have office/equipment)

**Timeline:** 1 week  
**Cost:** $1,000-$5,000/year
